About Nyay Mitra

Our Mission

Nyay Mitra is a revolutionary platform designed to bridge the justice gap in India. With over 45 million pending cases and 78% of citizens unaware of their legal rights, we aim to empower police officers, legal professionals, and citizens through innovative technology.

What We Do

We provide three dedicated portals:

  • Police Portal: Tools for officers to access records, automate FIRs, and analyze crime trends.
  • Lawyer Portal: Resources for legal professionals to generate documents and connect nationwide.
  • Citizen Portal: A multilingual chatbot to help citizens understand laws and find help.

With support for 22 languages, speech-to-text, and predictive analytics, Nyay Mitra is your friend in justice.
